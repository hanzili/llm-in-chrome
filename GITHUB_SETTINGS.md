## How to Set These

1. Go to https://github.com/hanzili/llm-in-chrome
2. Click **Settings** (gear icon) at the top right of the repository page
3. Under "About", click the gear icon to edit
4. Paste the **Description** above
5. Add the **Topics** (one at a time or comma-separated)
6. Click **Save changes**
